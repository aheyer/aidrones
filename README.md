This [document](https://github.com/christianbaun/aidrones/releases/latest/download/AI_Drones.pdf) provides an introduction to the complex topic of drones with artificial intelligence. Its focus is on the development (including the selection of suitable hardware and software components), construction, and operation of drones for teaching and research purposes.

The insights presented in this [document](https://github.com/christianbaun/aidrones/releases/latest/download/AI_Drones.pdf) stem from the research project *AI-Assisted Drone Platform*, funded by the [Connectom Networking and Innovation Fund](https://hessian.ai/de/forschung/connectom-vernetzungsfonds/) of hessian.AI, as well as from the course *Drones with Artificial Intelligence* ([WS2526](https://www.christianbaun.de/Master_Projekt_WS2526/index.html), [SS2025](https://www.christianbaun.de/Master_Projekt_SS2025/index_en.html)) at the Frankfurt University of Applied Sciences.

Key criteria for selecting the components presented in this [document](https://github.com/christianbaun/aidrones/releases/latest/download/AI_Drones.pdf) include adaptability to different application scenarios, cost, robustness, long-term market availability, and the quality of documentation and manufacturer support.

The goal of this [document](https://github.com/christianbaun/aidrones/releases/latest/download/AI_Drones.pdf) is not to provide an exhaustive treatment of drones and AI, but rather to focus on technologies and solutions that were current at the time of its creation and for which practical experience was gained in the Computer Science program of Faculty 2 (Computer Science and Engineering) at the Frankfurt University of Applied Sciences.
